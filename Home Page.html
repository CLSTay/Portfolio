What is Battle Royale?

Do you like to win?! Do you want to be the strongest fighter around?

Well now you can with Battle Royale, the latest 2-player arena fighter which has you and your friends fighting for the top place!

The game includes refined features such as...

  • Picturesque visuals created by an experienced Artist
  • Multiple weapons
  • Interesting and unique Map design
  • Great soundtrack unique to each stage

You can even play the Game within the theatre in June at Epping Forest College

Battle Royale is planned to be available for the Windows OS.
